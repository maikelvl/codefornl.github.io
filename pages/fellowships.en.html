Since 2014 we organize Fellowships at municipalities. You can register now for 2018!

One or more will run during a Code for NL fellowship professional designers and developers join a (semi-) government, to implement or make open source ICT solutions for social issues.

Such as: How can citizens gain insight into relevant information from their government? How do we organize participation at local level? How do we support people with a limitation in the use of public space?

Are you a designer or developer and would you like to have a half? year in a municipality or other government? Or am you are looking for ICT professionals on behalf of a municipality provide suitable digital solutions?

Sign up! Send an email to info@codefor.nl stating your name, contactdetails and a short motivation. We will contact you as soon as possible.
